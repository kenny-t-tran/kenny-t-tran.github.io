Industry Analysis: Fast-Food Restaurants in the U.S.

Capstone Project for Seminar in Strategic Management at CSUF

December 10, 2020

Our assignment was to analyze an industry of our choice and present our findings and recommendations for the industry's key players in a report and presentation. This project included writing up a performance analysis, Porter's industry analysis, and a key access factors analysis. We chose to examine the top five companies in the fast-food industry.

I was responsible for conducting the performance and profitability analysis, writing the executive summary, introduction, and conclusion, formatting the paper, and creating and designing the presentation slides. Our group received an A and the second-highest grade in the class.

Streetwear Retailer Marketing

Final Project for Principles of Marketing course at CSUF

April 23, 2019

The goal of this project was to analyze three services in an industry of our choice. The project included a discussion of each service's segmentation variables, marketing mix, marketplace positioning, SWOT, societal marketing and competitive advertising efforts, personal selling efforts, and supply chain management process.

I chose to analyze the streetwear retailing industry, focusing on the retailers Karmaloop, END. Clothing, and Barney's. Each retailer captures a different segment of the market, and I explore the differences in their approach to marketing. I received a grade of 100% on this project.
